About

Hello! My name is Amanda and I am the founder of Max Tutoring LLC. I have been tutoring and sharing my love of academics with students for over ten years. Max Tutoring offers a variety of services to include; home work help, one-on-one tutoring (in person and online), test prep, and summer retention. Students of all ages and skill levels welcome.

Services

Homework Assistance

Helping with homework on an as needed basis. For example: projects, reading assignments, etc.

One-on-One Tutoring

Weekly one-on-one tutoring for any subject area. This is typically done at the same time each week. It includes helping with homework and keeping the students up to speed or challenging them if the course work is not doing so.

Test Prep

Available for all major tests such as the ACT, SAT, and state assessments.

Summer Retention

Work on skills throughout the summer break to prepare for the upcoming school year while continuously reviewing the previous years skills.
